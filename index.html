Golden Retrievers

Golden Retrievers are one of the most common types of dogs because they are so cute, loving, and adorable. THey are very loyal to their owners are super friendly to outsiders too. These dogs can be a great family pet and a good working dog too. THey are amazing athletes, intelligent, and social dogs. Golden Retrievers are used as therapy dogs and they are the 4th smartest dog breed. Golden Retrievers make up the perfect combination of cute, freindly, playful, loyal, andd snuggly.
